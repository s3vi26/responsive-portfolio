About Me

My name is Nicole Sevillano, but mostly everyone calls me by my nickname Sevi! I am a web developer! I am was born and raised in Los Angeles county. I attended Santa Fe High School in Santa Fe Springs, CA and graduated in 2007. I majored in Political Science at SDCC.

In 2012, I moved to Boise, Idaho for almost 6 years. While I was in Idaho I started working for T-mobile. I loved working for the company, but moreover, I enjoyed working under our CEO John Legere. My biggest hobbies would be playing online PC video games or at the beach playing volleyball.
